This spawns a Weapon item in the game.

Weapons have a type, position, initial x/y velocity, time and various properties. The properties are bitmasked onto a char. Valid Properties:

  • usesVelocity
  • usesGravity
  • usesTimer
  • usesHoming
  • usesMovement
  • usesController
Parameters
typea char storing the type of weapon item this is, as defined by the enumeration Weapons.
xthe starting x position of the weapon
ythe starting y position of the weapon
xVelocitythe starting x velocity of the weapon
yVelocitythe staarting y velocity of the weapon
timeweapons use time for different purposes, (e.g. fuse length)
propertiesa char that is used as a bitmasked variable for the types of properties this weapon requires
